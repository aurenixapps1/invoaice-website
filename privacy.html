Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information InvoAIce collects, how we use it, how we protect it and the choices available to you.

Last Updated: June 2026

Overview

InvoAIce is developed by Aurenix Apps. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it and the choices available to you when using InvoAIce.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we may collect your unique user ID, name, email address, profile photo (if available), preferred currency, country, subscription status and account settings.


Business Information

We store the business profile you create, including your business name, address, phone number, email address, website, tax information, payment details and invoice preferences.


Invoice Data

We securely store invoices you create, including client information, invoice numbers, invoice items, prices, taxes, currencies, due dates, payment status, notes and attachments where applicable.


AI & Voice Input

When you use AI invoice generation or voice features, your prompts and voice transcriptions are securely processed to generate invoice drafts. Audio recordings are not permanently stored unless explicitly stated.

How We Use Information

App Functionality

Your information is used to create invoices, synchronize data across your devices, generate reports, export PDFs and CSV files, manage subscriptions and provide customer support.


AI Services

AI requests are processed only when you choose to use AI features. Information sent is limited to what is necessary to generate invoice content or suggestions.


Service Improvements

We may use anonymous diagnostics and crash reports to improve reliability, performance and security.

Third-Party Services

Authentication

User authentication is provided using Apple Sign In, Google Sign In and Firebase Authentication.


Cloud Storage

Your account information and invoices are securely stored using Google Firebase services.


AI Processing

AI invoice generation and writing assistance are powered by OpenAI. Only information required to process your request is transmitted.


Crash Reporting

Firebase Crashlytics may collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability.

Security

Data Security

We use encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections, secure authentication and industry-standard cloud infrastructure to help protect your information.


Data Retention

Your information remains stored while your account is active. Once your account is deleted, your data will be permanently removed from our systems after required processing is completed.

Your Rights

Access & Control

You can view and manage your invoices, business information and account settings at any time within the app.


Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account from the Settings screen. This removes your account, invoices, business information and associated data from our systems, subject to technical processing delays.

AI Disclaimer

AI-generated invoices and suggestions are provided as drafting assistance only. You are responsible for reviewing all invoice information, calculations, taxes, legal requirements and client details before sending an invoice.

Subscriptions

Premium features are available through auto-renewable subscriptions managed by Apple. Billing, renewals and refunds are handled according to Apple's App Store policies.

Additional Information

Children's Privacy

InvoAIce is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable laws.


International Users

Your information may be processed in countries where our trusted service providers operate. Appropriate safeguards are used to protect your data.


Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be reflected by updating the Last Updated date.
