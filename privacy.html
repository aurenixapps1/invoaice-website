Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Aurenix Apps ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how InvoAIce collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our application.


2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use InvoAIce, we may collect:

  • Name and email address (when signing in)
  • Invoice information you create
  • Business profile information
  • Subscription status
  • Application preferences and settings
  • Voice input used for AI invoice generation (only when you choose to use voice features)

3. Authentication

You may sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign-In. Authentication is securely managed through Firebase Authentication. We do not have access to your passwords.


4. Invoice Data

Invoices, clients, business information, and settings are securely stored in your personal Firebase Cloud Firestore database. Your data belongs to you.


5. AI Features

When you use AI invoice generation, the information you provide may be securely processed by our AI provider to generate invoice content. Only the information necessary to complete your request is processed.


6. Voice Input

Voice recordings are processed only after you explicitly start voice input. Speech recognition is used solely to convert your speech into invoice information.


7. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, InvoAIce may send reminders for invoice due dates, overdue invoices, and payment tracking.


8. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.


9. Analytics

We may collect anonymous diagnostic information to improve reliability, performance, and user experience. No invoice content is used for advertising.


10. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted communication and secure cloud infrastructure provided by Firebase.


11. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your information
  • Update your information
  • Delete your account
  • Delete all stored invoice data
  • Stop using cloud synchronization at any time

12. Account Deletion

Deleting your account from within InvoAIce permanently removes your authentication account together with your associated cloud data, subject to technical limitations and legal obligations.


13. Children's Privacy

InvoAIce is not intended for children under the age of 13.


14. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective immediately after publication.
