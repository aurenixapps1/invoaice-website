Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information InvoAIce collects, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

📋 Introduction

Aurenix Apps ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how InvoAIce collects, stores, uses, and protects your information when you use our application and related services.

📦 Information We Collect

Depending on how you use InvoAIce, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address when signing in.
  • Invoice information you create.
  • Business profile information.
  • Invoice preferences and application settings.
  • Your subscription status.
  • Voice input used for AI invoice generation when you choose to use voice features.

🔐 Authentication

You may sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign-In. Authentication is securely managed using Firebase Authentication. We never have access to or store your account passwords.

☁️ Invoice Data

Your invoices, clients, business information, preferences, and application settings are securely stored within your personal Firebase Cloud Firestore database. Your information belongs to you.

🤖 AI Features

When you use AI invoice generation, the information you provide may be securely processed by our AI service provider solely for the purpose of generating invoice content. Only the information required to complete your request is processed.

🎤 Voice Input

Voice recordings are processed only after you explicitly start voice input. Speech recognition is used solely to convert your spoken words into invoice information and is never activated without your permission.

🔔 Notifications

If you allow notifications, InvoAIce may send reminders about invoice due dates, overdue invoices, and payment tracking. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

💳 Subscriptions

Paid subscriptions are securely processed through Apple's App Store. We do not receive, collect, or store your payment card information.

📊 Analytics

We may collect anonymous diagnostic and performance data to improve reliability, fix bugs, and enhance the user experience. Your invoice content is never used for advertising purposes.

🛡️ Data Security

We protect your information using industry-standard security practices, encrypted communication, secure cloud infrastructure, and Firebase security features to help safeguard your data.

✅ Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Update or correct your information.
  • Delete your account.
  • Delete your invoice data.
  • Stop using cloud synchronization.

🗑️ Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account from within InvoAIce. When your account is deleted, your authentication account and associated cloud data are permanently removed, subject to technical limitations and any legal obligations.

👶 Children's Privacy

InvoAIce is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

📝 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or business practices. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated revision date.

📧 Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us.
