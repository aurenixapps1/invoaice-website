❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an invoice?

Tap the "+" button on the Home screen and create an invoice manually or let AI generate one from text or voice.

How do AI invoices work?

Describe your invoice naturally and InvoAIce generates invoice details automatically. Always review generated content before sending it to clients.

Where is my data stored?

Invoices, business information and settings are securely stored using Firebase Cloud Firestore when Cloud Sync is enabled.

📄 More Questions

Can I export invoices as PDF?

Yes. Every invoice can be exported as a professional PDF that's ready to share with your clients.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deleting your account permanently removes your authentication account together with your associated cloud data.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the subscription screen and tap Restore Purchases while signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase your subscription.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is protected using Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, encrypted communication, and industry-standard security practices.
