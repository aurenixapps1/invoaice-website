Frequently Asked Questions


How do I create an invoice?

Tap the + button on the Home screen and either create an invoice manually or use AI to generate one from a description or voice input.


How do AI invoices work?

Describe your invoice in natural language, and InvoAIce will generate invoice details for you. Always review generated content before sending it to clients.


Where is my data stored?

Your invoices and business information are securely stored using Firebase Cloud Firestore when cloud sync is enabled.


Can I export invoices as PDF?

Yes. InvoAIce allows you to export professional PDF invoices that can be shared with your clients.


How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated cloud data.


How do I restore my subscription?

Open the subscription screen and tap Restore Purchases while signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase your subscription.
